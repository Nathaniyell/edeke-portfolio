E-commerce Skincare Website (2022 & 2024)

In 2022, I embarked on a journey to design an e-commerce website for a skincare brand. As a UI/UX designer, my focus was to create a platform that could not only sell products but also educate and engage users about skincare routines tailored to their needs. However, at the time, I did not fully consider key design principles such as alignment, hierarchy, color theory, and font choices. In 2024, I revisited the website, applying a deeper understanding of these concepts to create a more polished and engaging user experience. This case study explores my thought process, challenges, and solutions across both versions.

Project Timeline

First Design: 2022

Redesign: 2024

Understanding the Brand and Users (2022)

Before diving into design, I invested time in understanding the skincare brand’s identity and its core audience. The target demographic for the website was primarily women aged 20-45, who are health-conscious, tech-savvy, and eager to invest in skincare solutions that suit their specific skin types and issues.

User Research:

I conducted surveys and interviews with potential users to discover pain points and desires in online skincare shopping:

Pain Points:

  • Users struggled with overwhelming product options.
  • They lacked guidance in choosing products for their skin concerns.

Desires:

  • A seamless and personalized shopping experience.
  • Information on skincare ingredients.

Based on this research, I knew the website had to be simple, educational, and visually appealing to users. However, I lacked a strong understanding of how to apply alignment, hierarchy, colors, and font pairings, which later became key areas for improvement in 2024.

First Version (2022) – Initial Approach and Design Decisions

User Research:

Goals:

The main goals were to create a user-friendly interface and smooth navigation for product exploration and purchasing. In this version, I emphasized:

  • Minimalistic Layout: I focused on a clean and minimalist aesthetic to avoid overwhelming users.
  • Simple Navigation: I kept the menu straightforward, with categories like Body lotion, Body wash, Body scrub, Organic oils, based on popular product types.

However, in this first version, I had limited understanding of key UI/UX principles. I did not fully consider alignment, which led to inconsistent spacing in the layout. My use of hierarchy was not well-defined, making it difficult for users to quickly navigate the content. Color choices were neutral, but I had not yet mastered how to use them to guide attention or create contrast. Similarly, my font selection was simplistic and did not create a strong visual identity for the brand.

Redesign (2024) – A More Immersive and Personalized Experience

In 2024, I revisited the project with the goal of improving the user experience by applying a deeper understanding of alignment, hierarchy, colors, and fonts. The focus was on creating a more cohesive, engaging, and personalized design that could better reflect the brand’s identity while addressing the evolving needs of users.

Updated Goals:

  • Hyper-Personalization: Users should feel that the website understands their unique skincare needs.
  • Enhanced Visual Appeal: Modernize the aesthetic with richer, bolder visuals while maintaining a clean design that now applied clear alignment and hierarchy.
  • Increased Engagement: Create opportunities for users to interact with the brand and each other.

Key improvements:

  • Virtual Consultations: I integrated an option for virtual skincare consultations to help users make informed purchasing decisions.
  • Dynamic Content: Regularly updated blog content on skincare routines, tips, and ingredients to keep users engaged and coming back for more.

Visual Enhancements:

  • Improved Alignment and Hierarchy: In the 2024 version, I paid close attention to alignment, ensuring that all elements were consistently spaced and aligned properly across devices. I also improved the hierarchy, clearly differentiating headings, subheadings, body text, and CTAs (call-to-action buttons) to create a smoother content flow.
  • Bolder and Intentional Use of Colors: I introduced more vibrant color accents, especially for buttons and key sections like promotional banners, to guide user attention effectively. I also applied color theory to create contrast and visual interest while maintaining the calming, wellness-oriented vibe of the brand.
  • Font Choices: I revamped the typography, choosing modern, elegant fonts that created a luxurious feel without sacrificing readability. I used a clear font hierarchy to differentiate between headings, body text, and CTAs, making the content more scannable and visually appealing.
  • High-Quality Imagery: The redesign featured larger product images and lifestyle photos to enhance the visual appeal, creating a more immersive experience for users

Challenges And Solutions

Challenge 1: Balancing Minimalism with Rich User Experience

In the 2022 version, I leaned heavily into a minimalist aesthetic, which made navigation easy but also left users feeling disconnected from the brand’s personality. In 2024, I introduced richer content and more interactive elements while maintaining simplicity through a strategic layout. My improved understanding of alignment and hierarchy allowed me to introduce more content without overwhelming users.

Challenge 2: Keeping Users Engaged

With increasing competition in the skincare e-commerce space, it was essential to build a sense of community. The introduction of user-generated content, like reviews with images, helped create an ecosystem where users could learn and share their experiences, fostering loyalty and repeat visits. Here, consistent alignment and a clear font hierarchy helped keep user-generated content organized and easy to interact with

Results and Impact

Improved Aesthetic and Usability: The redesigned version was visually more appealing due to the improved alignment, hierarchy, color use, and typography, contributing to a more polished and professional user experience.

Conclusion

Welcome to The redesign journey of the e-commerce skincare website from 2022 to 2024 was a rich learning experience. By embracing user feedback, sharpening my UI skills—especially in alignment, hierarchy, color, and typography—and staying current with design trends, I was able to transform a simple e-commerce site into a vibrant, personalized platform that catered to users' evolving needs. This project strengthened my ability to design solutions that not only look good but also function intuitively, making it a key highlight in my portfolio as I pursue new opportunities in UI/UX design.
